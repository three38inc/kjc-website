BBA Admission Processes

All aspiring candidates are requested to apply online for the Admission. Result awaiting candidates can apply for the course with plus one marks and update the online application forms once the plus two marks are available. Counseling date will be announced in college website and will be intimated through the respective E-mail ids provided in application form.

Every applicant for BBA programme has to go through the counseling (group discussion and personal interview with admission panel) conducted in college. Admission to BBA program is based on their performance in qualifying exam and the above counseling.

Selected applicants in the interview will be informed about the reporting date on the day of counselling. Admission formalities of submitting all available original documents and fees for the first year must be completed on or before the reporting date. Otherwise, their admission will be cancelled.
