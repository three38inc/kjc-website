Research Grant - Shodh Pravartan

Application Process

  • Application for the grant has to be submitted in the prescribed format to the Centre for Research as an email attachment to research@kristujayanti.com
  • The application for Minor / Major research grant comprises of: i) Application (Proforma I A and B) ; ii) Research Proposal (Proforma II); iii) Budget allocation (Proforma III); iv) Undertaking (Proforma IV)
  • Proforma I A for Principal Investigator and Proforma IB for Co-investigator have to be submitted separately. However, the other Proformas may just be one per project.
  • Faculty members can apply for the research grant collaboratively, jointly or individually.
  • The researchers may forge inter-departmental collaboration through their projects.

Selection Process

  • The research proposals would be reviewed by a panel of experts.
  • Selection of the proposal would be objectively based on the relevance, innovativeness and social significance of the project.
